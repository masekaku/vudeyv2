Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how THE ARTIFACT VIDEY collects, uses and protects information.

Data We Collect

  • Local storage: click counts, browsing history (last 5 items).
  • Analytics: anonymized events sent to configured analytics providers (GA4, Histats).
  • Disqus comments (hosted by Disqus) are subject to Disqus policies.

Cookies & Tracking

We use third-party analytics; these providers may use cookies. You can opt-out by disabling third-party scripts in your browser.

User Rights

You can request removal of locally stored data by clearing your browser storage or contacting us via the contact page. We do not store personal information on our servers by default.
