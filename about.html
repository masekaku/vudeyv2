About THE ARTIFACT VIDEY

THE ARTIFACT VIDEY is a curated directory of video artifacts from across multiple platforms. Our mission is to surface interesting visual artifacts and make them easy to discover.

Mission

To provide a lightweight, privacy-conscious, and aesthetically pleasing directory of video artifacts for explorers and archivists.

Tech

Terminal-style UI, Space Mono font, PWA + offline, client-side search & pagination, analytics placeholders.
